Estimated Market Value

The estimated market value (EMV) represents the anticipated selling price of the items in question. It is an approximation based on current market trends, demand, and other relevant factors that influence the value of the items being sold.

Repair Cost

The repair cost refers to the expenses incurred to restore the items before they can be sold.

Custom Shares

By clicking the "set" button, you can define custom shares for each participant involved in the loot production. This allows for a more tailored distribution of costs and profits based on individual contributions or agreements among the participants.

Participants

The number of participants indicates how many players were involved loot production. This number can affect the distribution of costs and profits among those involved.

Bags

The number of bags produced in a content

Discount

The discount percentage represents any reduction applied to the total cost or price. This can be used as an incentive for buyers or to account for negotiations during the sale.

General Information

This calculator is designed to Albion players estimate the final cost of selling items after accounting for various factors such as repair costs, number of participants, bags used, and applicable discounts. By inputting the relevant data, users can quickly determine the net amount they can expect from their sales.
